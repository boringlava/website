About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Geosciences at Stony Brook University in Stony Brook, New York. My dissertation focuses on clumped isotope paleoclimate records from Miocene soil carbonates, from the Turkana Basin, Kenya. I am a member of the Henkes Lab and a collaborator on the Turkana Miocene Project.
